Explaination¶

random under sample with and without hyper parameter tuning and ros default model almost same accuracy but much higher then raw data model

accuracy is the highest and also the recall is 97 much higher so we find this model our tuned model of ros in beating or performing better. this is the highest accuracy we are getting not only because of using a random oversmaple but also using standardization,preprocessing and proper endcoding of our data and adopting the good hyper parameter for tuning so accuracy is 92 and the precision and recall is also very high
